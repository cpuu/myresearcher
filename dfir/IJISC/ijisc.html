IJISC

(International Journal of Information Security and Cybercrime)

IJISC – International Journal of Information Security and Cybercrime is a scientific peer-reviewed journal, founded in 2012 and published by RAISA – Romanian Association for Information Security Assurance. The journal is edited by professorial staff and experts from “Alexandru Ioan Cuza” Police Academy, University Politehnica of Bucharest and General Inspectorate of Romanian Police.
